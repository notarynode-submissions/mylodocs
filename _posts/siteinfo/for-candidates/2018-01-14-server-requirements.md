**Hardware**

CPU: Xeon/i7

RAM: 64 GB

Disk: Minimum of 500GB SSD (1 TB recommended)

**Operating System**

Ubuntu x64 - 14.04 or 16.04 minimal installation with Openssh server

**Internet Bandwidth**

Minimum of 100 Mbps
